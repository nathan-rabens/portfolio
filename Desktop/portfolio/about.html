Short Bio

Nathan was born and raised in the northern suburbs of Detroit. In 2011 he graduated from West Bloomfield High School. He continued his education at Michigan State University, where he walked onto the Mens Club Lacrosse Team his Freshman Year. In 2012 he became a member of the Alpha Tau Omega Fraternity where he explored business opportunities and social gatherings. Nathan gained admitance to the Eli Broad College of Business in early 2013. By this time Nathan had taken multiple leadership roles within the fratenrity such as, social chairmen and pledge educator. In December of 2015, Nathan graduated from Michigan State University with a Bachelor of Arts in Supply Chain Management. Today Nathan continues to improve his education, while focusing on his professional career.
